# CSR Study Design Workflow Rules

## Source hierarchy
1. SOURCE_TEXT = primary ground truth
2. STRUCTURED_ELEMENTS = secondary source-derived representation
3. OPTIONAL_STYLE_GUIDANCE = style only, never factual authority

## Core rule
Do not introduce unsupported study design content.

## Support categories
- supported
- weakly supported
- unsupported

## Materiality categories
- major
- moderate
- minor

## Applicability rule
Only include or expect elements that are supported and applicable.

## Style guidance rule
OPTIONAL_STYLE_GUIDANCE may guide tone and structure only.
It must not contribute study facts.

## Output philosophy
Prefer omission over unsupported inference.
Prefer factual safety over polished completeness.
